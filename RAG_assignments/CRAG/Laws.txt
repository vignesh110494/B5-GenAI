Section	Description	Common Use / Application
Section 124A	Sedition	Used for acts that bring hatred or contempt toward the Government.
Section 302	Punishment for Murder	Applied in cases where a person intentionally causes the death of another.
Section 304B	Dowry Death	Used when a woman dies under abnormal circumstances related to dowry harassment.
Section 307	Attempt to Murder	Applied when an act is done with the intent to kill, but the victim survives.
Section 319	Hurt	Used for causing bodily pain, disease, or infirmity to any person.
Section 354	Outraging the Modesty of a Woman	Applied in cases of assault or criminal force against a woman's dignity.
Section 375/376	Rape / Punishment for Rape	Used for cases involving non-consensual sexual intercourse.
Section 378/379	Theft / Punishment for Theft	Applied when movable property is taken dishonestly without consent.
Section 390/392	Robbery / Punishment for Robbery	Used when theft involves causing fear of instant death or hurt.
Section 406	Criminal Breach of Trust	Applied when someone misappropriates property entrusted to them.
Section 415/420	Cheating / Fraud	Used for dishonestly inducing a person to deliver property or money.
Section 498A	Cruelty by Husband or Relatives	Applied in cases of domestic violence or harassment of a married woman.
Section 499/500	Defamation	Used when someone harms the reputation of another through words or signs.
